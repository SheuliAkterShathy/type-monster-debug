Answer: The difference between sessionStorage and localStorage is that localStorage data does not expire, whereas sessionStorage data is cleared when the page session ends.
Data is not available for future sessions in SessionStorage whereas data is available for future sessions in localStorage.Saves data in key/value pairs in the browser for both storage.
